Description of Service

The App is a mobile application designed to help parents and guardians track their children's height growth over time. The App provides:

  • Height measurement recording and tracking for multiple children
  • Growth visualization through interactive charts
  • Growth percentile comparisons using WHO and CDC reference data
  • Adult height predictions using growth percentile projection, mid-parental height calculation, and combined methods
  • Growth velocity analysis with monthly and annual growth rate tracking
  • Height milestone detection and celebration
  • AR-based height measurement using device camera and sensors
  • Photo timeline, gallery, comparison, and slideshow features
  • Sibling growth comparison charts
  • PDF growth reports and CSV data export
  • Local backup and restore of all app data
  • Customizable measurement reminder notifications
