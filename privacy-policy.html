Privacy Policy for HeightTracker

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Overview

HeightTracker ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection and Storage

What We Collect

HeightTracker collects and stores the following information that you provide:

  • Child profiles (names, birth dates, biological sex)
  • Height measurements and dates
  • Photos you choose to attach to measurements
  • Parent height information (for prediction features)
  • Reminder preferences

Where Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device. HeightTracker does not transmit any personal information to external servers. Your children's data never leaves your device.

  • Data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Photos are stored locally in the app's secure container
  • No cloud sync or backup to third-party servers

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data remains on your device
  • The app uses iOS file protection for secure storage
  • No network connections are made to transmit personal data
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requests access to:

  • Camera: To capture photos of your child for measurements (optional)
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos (optional)

These permissions are only used when you choose to add photos. Photos are stored locally and are never uploaded to any server.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App uses local notifications to remind you to take measurements. This does not require sharing any data externally.

Children's Privacy

HeightTracker is designed to help parents track their children's growth. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • No data is collected from children directly
  • All data entry is performed by the parent/guardian
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No advertising is displayed

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device as long as you use the App
  • Deleting a child profile permanently removes all associated data
  • Uninstalling the App removes all stored data
  • You have complete control over your data at all times

Third-Party Services

HeightTracker does not use any third-party services that collect user data:

  • No analytics platforms
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: heighttracker.app@gmail.com

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in the App
  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • Export your data (via the App's export feature)
  • Uninstall the App to remove all data

HeightTracker is not affiliated with any healthcare providers and is intended for personal tracking purposes only. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.
